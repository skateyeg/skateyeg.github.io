Our Local Culture

There is a culture behind this project. It stems from the local rollerblading scene and we would like to pay homage to Shredmonton Inline - without the influence of this group there would be no Book of Grinds.

Shredmonton Inline Skating Association is a registered non-profit organization who's focus is on growing inline skating in the Edmonton capital region.

Check out Shredmonton Inline @

Instagram - clips, pics, stories on WNS locations, etc.

Facebook - local chat, discussions, session info, etc.

Website - merch, event entries, etc.
