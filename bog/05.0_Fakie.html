Grinds - Variations - Fakie

*Grind previews are left leg dominant.*

Description

Definition: Fakie can be applied to any grind. You can fakie into or out of tricks (or both). Essentially fakie is when you roll up to a trick backwards and either jump into it backwards or spin into it from rolling backwards. Fakie out would be if you exit your trick either by jumping out (if you're already going in reverse) or by spinning out and landing backwards.
