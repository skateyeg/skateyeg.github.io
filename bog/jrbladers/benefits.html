Program Benefits

Consider some of these core concepts children can learn and experience with us. See why this type of rollerblading and the aggressive inline program is a good path for your child to take!

Why Skate Parks and Aggressive Inline?

  • Aggressive Inline can be the next step in your child's rollerblading journey and open up a new world of possibilities and abilities.
  • The challenges become bigger, and harder, and are practically limitless, there is always something new to learn or try.
  • It adds new types of physical training required to perform tricks and use obstacles, it's a full body workout that will engage all sorts of muscles.
  • It creates pathways for mental development.
  • Aggressive Inline has a fantastic and supportive community that is felt locally, as well as internationally. We're like one big family.

Mental Benefits

  • Learn self discipline by being exposed to challenges one must overcome by thinking and acting in a specific manner all while being around other people.
  • Conquer fear and pushing boundaries of ones' own limits while learning how to use obstacles, jumping on and off of objects.
  • Promote higher level of thinking while planning and debating movements, assessing risk of performing actions, and exploring consequences of these actions.
  • Learn consistency that is required to land tricks while using rollerblades.
  • Provide an opportunity to learn patience while learning or teaching others.
  • Always-present feeling of self-worth when succeeding at a given task or overcoming a challenge.
  • Self-expression through movement and style and using ones' imagination and confidence.

Physical Benefits

  • Muscle strength including many different muscle groups.
  • Physical balance i.e. the act of balancing.
  • Exercise that's beyond team sports, or fitness rollerblading.
  • Elevated levels of stamina.
  • Muscle consistency and flow of muscle movement (smoothness).
  • It's like a martial art, but on wheels.

Social Benefits

  • Children participate in a group activity that has both structure and freedom of creativity that can be directly shared with others.
  • We are a tight and friendly community of people with the common love for rollerblading.
  • We work as a team to achieve common or personal goals.
  • Children benefit from a sense of belonging.

Other Benefits

  • Training specific muscle groups creates and easy transition between rollerblading, ice skating, skiing etc.
  • Confidence that is transferrable between different sports and life in general.
  • Skating is something to fall back on to clear ones' mind and heart, to go and express oneself and release negative energy.
  • There is a variety of competitive skatepark and street skate events to attend! There are some locally, and many more internationally.
  • Life long friendships are built no matter on where people end up working, where they end up living.
  • Potential for future sponsor deals and exposure to a large international industry.
  • A common activity among adult (or sometimes younger) aggressive bladers is traveling the world to skate events with people who care for and look out for one another.
